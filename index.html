Mely's Janitorial Services offers general janitorial services in Texas.


From industrial buildings, medical facilities, schools, restaurants to retail stores, Mely's Janitorial Services focuses on learning each client's cleaning objectives to provide quality service customized to meet their needs. We work alongside our clients to guide them through their commercial cleaning projects. We strive to build lasting relationships with clients, consultants, and vendors through quality cleaning services, customer service, and transparency.
